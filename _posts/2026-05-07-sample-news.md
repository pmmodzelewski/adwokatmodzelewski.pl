This is a **sample news item**.

News items are usually shorter than blog posts — they cover current
information about dates, announcements, and changes in how the firm operates.

To add a new news item, create a file in the `_aktualnosci/` directory
named in the `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md` format. The structure is identical
to a blog post.

This sample item can be deleted once the first real news entry is added.
