About
Experience and education

In 2013 I graduated with distinction from the Faculty of Law and Administration at the University of Warsaw. My master's thesis on substantive criminal law was supervised by Dr Piotr Kładoczny.

I have been a member of the Warsaw Bar Association since 2017. For more than a decade I have represented Clients in litigation before courts of all instances — including the Supreme Court, the Administrative Courts and the Military Courts — in matters of broadly understood civil law (including family, inheritance and labour cases) as well as criminal law.

A particular area of my practice is the representation of professional soldiers and other uniformed services personnel (including officers of the Police, Prison Service, Border Guard, Internal Security Agency, Military Intelligence Service and Military Counterintelligence Service). I represent them in administrative, social-security (pensions and other benefits), disciplinary and criminal proceedings.

Practice areas
Areas of practice

Criminal law

Defence in criminal, petty-offence and fiscal-criminal cases, as well as representation of injured parties and auxiliary or private prosecutors.

Civil law

Payment claims, damages, infringement of personal rights, property law and contract law.

Family law

Representation in divorce, alimony, parental authority, place-of-residence and contact cases, as well as in property matters (division of marital assets).

Inheritance law

Proceedings for confirmation of inheritance acquisition and division of the estate, claims for the legitime (zachowek) and related matters.

Labour law

Representation of both employees (claims for payment, unlawful termination of employment, work certificates) and employers.

Uniformed services

All matters concerning professional soldiers and other uniformed services officers (administrative, pension, disciplinary, criminal, etc.).
