## Scope of Practice in Criminal Matters

I undertake the representation of clients in all types of criminal cases,
striving to meet their expectations to the highest possible standard.
I have appeared before:

- District Courts;
- Regional Courts (acting as courts of first and second instance);
- Courts of Appeal;
- Military Courts (Garrison and Regional);
- the Supreme Court (cassation proceedings and second-instance proceedings in military cases).

## Scope of Services

I represent clients (inter alia) in cases concerning the following categories of offences:

### Offences Against Life and Health

Including cases involving homicide and causing grievous bodily harm.

### Offences Against Property

Including proceedings concerning theft, misappropriation, robbery and aggravated theft, fraud, as well as trespass.

### Offences Against Traffic Safety

Including causing road traffic accidents and driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

### Offences Against Sexual Freedom and Decency

Including both offences directed against such freedom and cases involving possession of prohibited materials.

### Offences Against Honour and Physical Integrity

Including cases of defamation, insult, and violation of bodily integrity.

### Offences Against the Family and Guardianship

Including non-payment of maintenance, improper care, and domestic abuse.

### Offences Against the Reliability of Documents

In particular, document forgery, counterfeiting or alteration, and the use of forged or otherwise inauthentic documents.

### Offences Against the Activities of State Institutions

Including cases involving abuse of powers or failure to perform official duties.

### Offences Under the Act on Counteracting Drug Addiction

Including possession of narcotic drugs, as well as more serious offences such as distribution, trafficking, or manufacture.

### Offences Committed by Professional Soldiers under the Military Part of the Criminal Code

Including, inter alia, desertion, failure to obey orders, and abuse of subordinates.

### Fiscal Offences

Including issuing unreliable invoices and evasion of public-law liabilities.

### ...and others

## How to Get Started

In criminal proceedings, particularly those concerning more serious offences, the assistance of defense counsel or
a legal representative at the early stages is crucial. Professional support from the outset of proceedings—such
as at the stage of reporting an offence or the presentation of charges—may have a positive impact on the entire
course of the case. For this reason, it is advisable to seek legal assistance without delay.

You are encouraged to make contact by phone or email to arrange an initial consultation,
as well as in situations requiring urgent intervention (e.g. in the event of detention or a search).

Each case requires an individual assessment of the factual circumstances and the current procedural stage.
