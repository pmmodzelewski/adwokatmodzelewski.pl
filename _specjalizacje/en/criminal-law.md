## Practice in criminal matters

I represent Clients in criminal cases under both substantive and procedural
law — on the defence side as well as for injured parties. I appear before
courts of all instances, including the Supreme Court.

### Scope of services

- defence in pre-trial and trial proceedings,
- representation of injured parties, auxiliary and private prosecutors,
- fiscal-criminal cases,
- petty-offence cases,
- preventive measures (pre-trial detention, supervision, bans),
- cassations and motions to reopen proceedings.

## Experience

For more than a decade I have handled criminal cases of significant factual
and legal complexity. A particular focus of my practice is matters concerning
professional soldiers and other uniformed services personnel — including
both criminal liability and disciplinary proceedings.

## Getting started

Please feel free to reach out by phone or email to arrange an initial
consultation. Each case requires individual analysis of the facts and
the current procedural posture.
