## Practice in civil matters

I handle civil cases broadly — both substantive and procedural. I represent
Clients in payment disputes, damages cases, contract disputes, and property
law matters.

### Scope of services

- payment claims (invoices, loans, contractual penalties),
- damages and compensation (traffic, medical, other),
- infringement of personal rights,
- contract disputes and contract interpretation,
- property law (ownership, prescription, easements),
- drafting and reviewing civil contracts.

## How I work

Every case begins with an analysis of the facts and the legal situation
and a discussion of realistic options. Fees are agreed individually —
flat-rate, hourly, or success-fee arrangements are all possible.
