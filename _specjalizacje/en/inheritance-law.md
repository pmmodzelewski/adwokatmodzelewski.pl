## Practice in inheritance matters

In inheritance cases, knowledge of the procedure often determines the
final outcome. I handle both non-contentious proceedings (notarial and
judicial) and contested processes between heirs.

### Scope of services

- confirmation of inheritance (court order or notarial deed),
- division of the estate — judicial and contractual,
- legitime (zachowek) — claiming and defending,
- acceptance and rejection of inheritance, declarations on behalf of minors,
- wills — drafting and contesting,
- proceedings for declaration of unworthiness to inherit,
- cross-border estates (jurisdiction, applicable law).

## Time and cost

Inheritance proceedings can be lengthy — particularly division-of-estate
cases involving multiple heirs. From the first consultation I provide
a realistic time and cost framework.
