## Matters of soldiers and officers

A particular focus of my practice is matters concerning professional
soldiers and other uniformed services personnel — including officers of
the Police, Prison Service, Border Guard, Internal Security Agency, the
Foreign Intelligence Agency, Military Counterintelligence Service, and
Military Intelligence Service.

### Scope of services

- disciplinary proceedings (from charges through cassation),
- criminal and fiscal-criminal cases (including before military courts),
- administrative cases (dismissals, service evaluations),
- pension and benefit cases under the uniformed-services scheme,
- pay, allowances, equivalents,
- appeals concerning pay-grade classification,
- service-housing entitlement cases.

## Specifics of uniformed-services cases

These cases follow their own rules — from a distinctive disciplinary
procedure, through unique features of military criminal procedure, to
a separate pension system. Quick and accurate legal analysis from the
first days of proceedings can dramatically change their course.
