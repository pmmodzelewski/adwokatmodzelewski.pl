## Practice in labour matters

I represent both employees and employers. This is an area where speed of
response often matters — appeal deadlines for terminations are short and
strict.

### Services for employees

- appeals against notice of termination and against immediate dismissal,
- reinstatement or compensation,
- claims for unpaid wages, equivalents, overtime,
- mobbing, discrimination, infringement of personal rights at work,
- work certificate — correction and compensation.

### Services for employers

- drafting work, remuneration, and social fund regulations,
- compliance audits with labour-law requirements,
- representation in disputes initiated by employees,
- group-dismissal and restructuring matters.

## Deadlines

Labour-law deadlines can be unforgiving — for example, 21 days to appeal
against a termination notice. Please contact me as soon as possible after
receiving the disputed document.
